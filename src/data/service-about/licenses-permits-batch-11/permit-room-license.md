# Permit Room License

## Detailed Overview

A Permit Room License (often called a "Permit Room" in Maharashtra and similar designations in other states) is a state excise license authorising a restaurant, hotel, or eatery to serve all categories of liquor (beer, wine, IMFL spirits) for on-premises consumption with food. The "Permit Room" is essentially the licensed area within the restaurant where liquor service is permitted.

This is the most common type of bar/restaurant liquor license in India, allowing the establishment to operate as a full-service food and beverage outlet with comprehensive liquor offerings. It is typically held by mid-to-upscale restaurants, hotels, lounges, pubs, and dining establishments.

## Advantages / Benefits

- Full range of liquor service (beer, wine, spirits, cocktails)
- Higher revenue potential — liquor sales typically have 200–400% margins
- Attracts diverse customer demographics
- Enables differentiated dining experience
- Premium brand positioning
- Supports cocktail innovation and mixology

## Disadvantages / Limitations

- High annual license fees (₹5–25 lakh typically)
- Strict compliance — surprise inspections, age verification, dry days
- Heavy security deposits
- Operating hour restrictions
- Strict bookkeeping and stock register maintenance
- Heavy penalties for violations (license cancellation, prosecution)

## Features

- On-premises service with food
- All liquor categories covered
- Specific licensed area (Permit Room) within restaurant
- Annual validity
- Mandatory food sales requirement (typically minimum 30–40% of revenue)

## Documents Required

- Application form — State-specific
- Restaurant trade license — Municipal trade license
- FSSAI License — Food safety registration
- Premises ownership/lease — Title or lease
- Site plan — Marking permit room area
- Fire NOC — Mandatory
- Police NOC
- Local body NOC — Municipal approval
- Distance certificate — Compliance with distance norms
- GST registration
- ITR (3 years) — Of entity/proprietor
- Partnership deed/MoA — Constitutional documents
- Bank solvency certificate

## Process / Procedure

1. Step 1 — Submit application with all documents
2. Step 2 — Site inspection by Excise officer
3. Step 3 — Police verification of proprietor/partners
4. Step 4 — Public notice for objections (in some states)
5. Step 5 — License fee and security deposit payment
6. Step 6 — License issuance
7. Step 7 — Stock procurement and operations start

Timeline: 60–180 days

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Is FSSAI License mandatory for a Permit Room License?** Yes. Without a valid FSSAI license, a permit room license cannot be granted as food service is integral to the license requirements.
2. **What is the minimum food sales requirement?** Most states require 30–40% of revenue from food sales. Pure bars without food don't qualify for permit room licenses.
3. **Can I have a separate "ladies-only" section in my permit room?** Yes, this is permissible and even recommended in some states. It must be clearly demarcated and noted in the license.
4. **Are happy hours and discounted liquor offers permitted?** Yes, but pricing must comply with state-mandated minimum prices. Selling below MRP attracts penalties.
5. **Can I have live music or DJ in the permit room?** Yes, but additional licenses (Public Performance License from PPL/IPRS, Police Entertainment License, dance floor license if applicable) are needed.
