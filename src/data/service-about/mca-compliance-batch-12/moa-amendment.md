# MoA Amendment

## Detailed Overview

The Memorandum of Association (MoA) is the constitutional document of a company defining its name, registered office state, objects (main and ancillary), liability of members, capital structure, and subscriber details. Amendment of MoA is governed by Sections 13 and 4 of the Companies Act, 2013, and requires special resolution of shareholders for most clauses.

Common MoA amendments include:

- Object clause expansion (adding new business activities)
- Capital clause modification (along with SH-7 for capital increase)
- Name change (alongside other forms)
- Registered office state change

## Applicability

Applicable to all companies. Pan-India.

## Compliance Requirements

| Clause | Approval Required |

| Registered office state | Special resolution + RD approval |
| Objects | Special resolution |
| Liability | Special resolution |
| Capital | Ordinary resolution (or special as per AOA) |

## Process / Procedure

- Board approves proposed MoA changes
- EGM notice (21 days)
- EGM passes special resolution
- Pay stamp duty if applicable
- File MGT-14 within 30 days
- Update MoA copy
- Special procedures apply for name change, state change.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Can MoA objects be expanded without limits?** Practically yes — companies can add multiple ancillary objects. However, very broad object clauses can attract regulatory scrutiny. Best practice is to keep objects relevant to actual business intent.
2. **Is RoC approval needed for MoA amendments?** For most clauses (including object), MGT-14 filing is intimation only — no prior approval. Name change and state change require additional Central Government / RD approval.
3. **What happens to existing contracts if objects clause is amended?** Existing contracts within original objects remain valid. New activities under amended objects can commence post-amendment.
4. **Can a Section 8 company amend its objects?** Section 8 company MoA can be amended but with restrictions — non-profit nature, specified objects must be preserved. Amendments may require Central Government approval.
5. **Does MoA amendment affect existing PAN/GST registrations?** Name change requires PAN/GST update. Object change typically doesn't require PAN/GST change but may affect business categorisation in GST.
