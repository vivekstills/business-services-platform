# Close an LLP

## Detailed Overview

Closing an LLP in India can be done through:

- Strike-off — under LLP Act for defunct LLPs (Form 24)
- Voluntary winding up — by partners
- Compulsory winding up — by Tribunal/Court

The most common is strike-off via Form 24, available for LLPs that have not commenced business or have stopped business for at least 1 year.

## Applicability

Applicable to all LLPs wishing to close. Pan-India.

## Compliance Requirements

| Method | Pre-conditions |
| --- | --- |
| Strike-off (Form 24) | No business for 1+ year (or never commenced); no liabilities; all returns filed |
| Voluntary winding up | Resolution by all partners; appointed liquidator |
| Compulsory winding up | Tribunal order; specific grounds |

## Benefits

- Clean closure with no future liabilities
- End of LLP compliance obligations
- Cost savings on annual filings

## Risks / Penalties

| Risk | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Pending compliance | Form 8, 11 must be up to date |
| Pending audit | Complete audit before closure |
| Active assets/liabilities | Settle and distribute before closure |

## Documents Required

| Document | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Form 24 | Strike-off application |
| Indemnity bond | From all partners |
| Affidavit | Confirming no business, no liabilities |
| Statement of accounts | Recent (within 30 days) |
| All pending Form 8 and Form 11 | Must be filed |
| Income tax returns | Up to date |
| Bank account closure proof |  |
| NOC from creditors | If any liabilities historically existed |

## Process / Procedure

- Verify eligibility (no business for 1+ year)
- Settle all liabilities and assets
- File pending Form 8, 11
- Close bank accounts
- Partners approve closure
- File Form 24 with attachments
- RoC publishes notice
- 30-day objection period
- Strike-off order if no objections

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Can an LLP be closed in the same year of incorporation?** No. Form 24 strike-off requires the LLP to not have commenced business for at least 1 year. Newly incorporated LLPs must wait or use other closure methods.
2. **What if my LLP has filed all returns but had minor business activity?** Even minor activity disqualifies Form 24 strike-off. Either continue and close after 1 year of inactivity, or pursue voluntary winding up.
3. **Can partners be held liable post-closure?** Indemnity bond signed by partners protects against future claims. However, if it later emerges that LLP had hidden liabilities, partners may face personal liability.
4. **Is closure allowed if LLP has pending tax disputes?** No. All tax matters must be settled. Pending IT proceedings must be concluded before closure.
5. **What happens to LLP's PAN after closure?** PAN technically continues but becomes inactive. Income Tax department is informed via the closure of LLP.
