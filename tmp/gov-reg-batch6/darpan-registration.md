# DARPAN registration (NGO DARPAN)

## Overview

**DARPAN (Database and Registration of Voluntary and Non-Profit Associations)** is an online portal from **NITI Aayog** that supports a **verified, searchable** national database of **NGOs, voluntary** organisations, and **civil society** entities in India. A **Unique DARPAN ID** is now often expected to access **central** grants, **FCRA** applications, and **CSR**-related work.

The portal is at **[ngodarpan.gov.in](https://ngodarpan.gov.in)**. It increases **transparency** and helps genuine organisations show registration status, leadership, and activity data to **ministries, donors, and** the public.

## Key features

- **Unique DARPAN ID** for each listed organisation.
- **Public** profile: organisation, leadership, and project fields as reported.
- **Prerequisite** for many **centre** grant and **FCRA** processes (check current circulars for your year).
- **CSR** and institutional donors use DARPAN as a basic visibility layer.
- **Covers** societies, **trusts**, **Section 8** companies, and other eligible non-profits.
- **No government fee** for **registration** on the portal in the normal flow.

## Benefits

- **Grants and schemes** that list DARPAN as a gate, subject to the ministry.
- **FCRA** and **P.P.** applications often need the ID; confirm the **MHA** portal for the live rule set.
- **CSR** channelling and **due diligence** by companies.
- **Credibility** when profile and projects are **kept** current.
- **Discoverability** for **implementation** partnerships.
- **De-duplication** and basic **verification** in government systems.
- A **stable digital** identity across applications.

## Limitations

- **Not** a quality or impact **rating;** it is a database entry, not a seal of “best NGO.”
- **DARPAN alone** does not guarantee any **grant;** each scheme has more rules.
- **Profile** maintenance is needed; old data can **hurt** new applications.
- **Not a substitute** for **FCRA**; FCRA is a **separate**, heavier process.
- **State** grants may use **other** state portals; DARPAN is **central-leaning**.
- **No automatic** funding; you still need to qualify each scheme on merit and documents.

## Process

1. **Open** [ngodarpan.gov.in](https://ngodarpan.gov.in) and **Sign up** for the organisation.
2. **Enter** legal name, type, registration number, state, **PAN.**
3. **Upload** registration certificate, **PAN**, and governing body and **contact** data as prompted.
4. **Complete** mission, **sectors,** geography, projects, and **bank** where required.
5. **NITI Aayog** (or the workflow) may **verify**; complete **e-mail/OTP** steps if asked.
6. **Unique ID** is issued after successful **verification.**
7. **Update** profile **yearly** (or as needed) for grants and **FCRA-** class uses.

## Applicability

**Registered** **societies, trusts, Section 8** companies, and similar **non-profits** that need **centre** grants, **FCRA,** **CSR**-aligned work, or **empanelment** with bodies that list DARPAN. **All states and UTs**; use as **in addition to** your **state** registration and sector licences.

## Validity

The **DARPAN ID** does not **expire** like a licence, but a **dormant** or **stale** profile can be **less** accepted for **GFR**-class processes. **Refresh** at least **annually** and after **governance** changes. **NITI Aayog** may **flag** incomplete or long-idle **records**.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is it mandatory for every NGO?** It is **mandatory** in practice when a **ministry, FCRA, or** a **funder** says so. **Local-only, unfunded** entities may not file, but the ID is now **widespread** for **credible** **central-**facing work.

**Can a new trust apply the day after CoI?** **Yes**; there is no **long** “years of work” **rule** for **DARPAN** itself. A **new** org may have **light** project text until it builds a track record; **update** the profile as you grow.

**Does DARPAN auto-enrol** us in **grants?** **No.** It is a **prerequisite** for some **pipelines,** not an **enrolment** in a **scheme** by itself. Each **ministry** has its own **eligibility, empanelment, and** **audit** conditions.

**Key people changed. Must we** update? **Yes,** and align **RoC/Registrar** and **DARPAN** so **FCRA, CSR, and** **grant** checks do not see **mismatches**.

**Section 8** **company: same flow?** **Yes.** **CoI, MOA, AOA, board, and** **PAN** of the company; the **governing** / **board** data maps like a **society** trust board in the form.
