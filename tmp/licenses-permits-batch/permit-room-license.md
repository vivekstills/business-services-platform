# Permit Room License

## Short overview

A Permit Room License (often called a "Permit Room" in Maharashtra and similar designations in other states) is a state excise license authorising a restaurant, hotel, or eatery to serve all categories of liquor (beer, wine, IMFL spirits) for on-premises consumption with food. The "Permit Room" is essentially the licensed area within the restaurant where liquor service is permitted.

This is the most common type of bar and restaurant liquor license in India, allowing the establishment to operate as a full-service food and beverage outlet with comprehensive liquor offerings. It is typically held by mid-to-upscale restaurants, hotels, lounges, pubs, and dining establishments.

## Key features

- On-premises service with food
- All liquor categories covered
- Specific licensed area (Permit Room) within restaurant
- Annual validity
- Mandatory food sales requirement (typically minimum 30 to 40% of revenue)

## Benefits

- Full range of liquor service (beer, wine, spirits, cocktails)
- Higher revenue potential, liquor sales typically have 200 to 400% margins
- Attracts diverse customer demographics
- Enables differentiated dining experience
- Premium brand positioning
- Supports cocktail innovation and mixology

## Limitations

- High annual license fees (₹5 to 25 lakh typically)
- Strict compliance, surprise inspections, age verification, dry days
- Heavy security deposits
- Operating hour restrictions
- Strict bookkeeping and stock register maintenance
- Heavy penalties for violations (license cancellation, prosecution)

## Process

1. Submit application with all documents.
2. Site inspection by Excise officer.
3. Police verification of proprietor or partners.
4. Public notice for objections (in some states).
5. License fee and security deposit payment.
6. License issuance.
7. Stock procurement and operations start.

Timeline: 60 to 180 days.

## Validity and renewal

Annual validity (financial year). Renewal 30 to 60 days before expiry. Compliance audit during renewal. Late renewal penalty: 25 to 100% additional.

## Frequently asked questions

**1. Is FSSAI License mandatory for a Permit Room License?** Yes. Without a valid FSSAI license, a permit room license cannot be granted as food service is integral to the license requirements.

**2. What is the minimum food sales requirement?** Most states require 30 to 40% of revenue from food sales. Pure bars without food don't qualify for permit room licenses.

**3. Can I have a separate "ladies-only" section in my permit room?** Yes, this is permissible and even recommended in some states. It must be clearly demarcated and noted in the license.

**4. Are happy hours and discounted liquor offers permitted?** Yes, but pricing must comply with state-mandated minimum prices. Selling below MRP attracts penalties.

**5. Can I have live music or DJ in the permit room?** Yes, but additional licenses (Public Performance License from PPL or IPRS, Police Entertainment License, dance floor license if applicable) are needed.
