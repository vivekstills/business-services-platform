# OPC to public limited company conversion

## Overview

An **OPC** can become a **public limited company** when the goal is **public fund-raising**, a future **listing**, or a **broad** shareholder base. The route is often **two steps**: **OPC to private (INC-6)**, then **private to public (MGT-14 + INC-27 / articles)**, or a path your **RoC** accepts as **consolidated** in practice. **Direct** OPC to public is **possible in law**; in practice, **staged** conversion is **common**.

> [!WARNING] **Compliance jump**
> A public company carries the **heaviest** ongoing compliance: minimum members/directors, independent directors, secretarial/CSR/SEBI paths if **listed**, and more **disclosure**.

## Key features

- **Capital markets**: Access to **public** investors and, when listed, **BSE/NSE** visibility.
- **Share liquidity**: **Freer** transfer of shares (subject to law and **listing** if applicable).
- **Scale**: **Unlimited** shareholders; room for **institutional** and **retail** capital.

| Stage | Core filings (typical) |
|-------|-------------------------|
| OPC to private | **INC-6** with amended MOA/AOA; at least 2 Sh + 2 Dir |
| Private to public | **MGT-14** (special resolution) + **INC-27** + altered MOA/AOA |
| Commencement | **INC-20A** and other post-incorporation steps as applicable |

## Benefits

- **Public issue** and **prospectus** path (where applicable) and **larger** investor pool.
- **Brand** and **governance** perception with **lenders**, customers, and partners.
- **Path to listing** (SEBI, DRHP, merchant bankers) when you are ready, **separate** from the **conversion** itself.

> [!NOTE] **Limitations**
> **7** shareholders and **3** directors minimum; **independent** directors, **secretarial** audit, **CSR** (if thresholds), **SEBI LODR** if **listed**. **Listing** is **6 to 18+ months** of work and **cost**. IPO **founder** sales trigger **CGT**; **ESOPs** have **perquisite** and **sale** tax angles.

## Process

1. **Convert OPC to private limited**: **INC-6**, amended **MOA/AOA**, at least **2** shareholders and **2** directors.
2. **EGM** and **special resolution** to convert to **public**; file **MGT-14** within the prescribed window.
3. **File INC-27** (or the applicable alteration route) with **public** company **MOA/AOA**.
4. Appoint at least **7 shareholders** and **3 directors** (with **DIR-2/DIR-12** as needed) **before or with** the certificate.
5. **RoC** issues **public limited** **Certificate of Incorporation**; **CIN** continues through the chain of **form** changes.
6. **INC-20A** (commencement) if required; if **listing** is the goal, start **SEBI** and **exchange** work **separately**.

## Documents required

| Document | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Certificate of incorporation (OPC) | Existing |
| MOA & AOA (OPC) | Existing |
| Board resolution for conversion | Signed |
| Altered MOA & AOA | For public limited |
| INC-6 (OPC to pvt) | MCA filing |
| INC-27 (pvt to public) | MCA filing |
| MGT-14 (special resolution) | After shareholder approval |
| Financial statements (latest audited) | CA certified |
| DSC of all directors | Class 3 |
| NOC from creditors | Signed |
| Consent of new directors (DIR-2) | Minimum 3 as needed |
| PAN, Aadhaar of new directors | KYC |

## Tax impact (summary)

**OPC to public** through **same** **PAN/CIN** is **tax-neutral** at the **entity** level: **no** deemed transfer in the **standard** chain. **IPO** proceeds in the company are **not** business income. **Founders** selling in **IPO** have **CGT**; **ESOP** at **exercise** and **sale** follow **perquisite** and **CGT** rules.

## Applicability

**OPCs in India** (all major states, **RoC** as per **jurisdiction**). **Listing** is on **BSE/NSE** (and **SME** boards) with **nationwide** membership **rules**.

## Frequently asked questions

**Must OPC go private first?** **Law** allows **direct** paths in some cases; **practice** is often **two step**. Confirm with your **CS** and **RoC**.

**Need 7 shareholders at conversion?** **Yes** for a **valid** public company; plan **evidence** before the **public** certificate.

**SEBI** immediately? **SEBI/LODR** matters mainly when **listed** or doing **securities** activities; **unlisted** public has **no** same **obligations** as **listed**.

**Name on conversion?** **"Private"** is **removed**; **extra** name change may need **INC-24** and **separate** fees.

**Minimum capital for** IPO? **SME** boards use **paid-up** **bands** in **SEBI** rules, **not** a single static number, **read** the **current** **master** circular and **NSE/BSE** pages.
