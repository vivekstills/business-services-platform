# ITR-2 form filing

## Overview

ITR-2 is for individuals and HUFs without business or profession income but with more complex sources: multiple houses, capital gains, foreign income, directors who are not in business, unlisted shares, high income, or agricultural income above the ITR-1 limit. Wrong form selection draws defective return risk.

## When ITR-2 is used (summary)

Salary, more than one house, STCG and LTCG, other sources, NRI in scope, foreign income and Schedule FA, total income above ITR-1 cap, agricultural income over five thousand, director without business in many cases.

**Not** for business, profession, F and O, or presumptive ITR-4: use ITR-3 or 4 as the case is.

## Key features

- Schedule CG, OS, and foreign schedules.
- Schedule AL when total income is above the notified threshold.
- FSI, TR, FA and other parts as the software asks for the year.

## Benefits

- One form for many investment and NRI fact patterns; loss carry in CG rules; DTAA credit rows where applicable.

## Documents

Form 16, broker and MF gains, property papers, 26AS, AIS, foreign bank and tax proofs, TRC, passport for NRI, loan and rent proofs.

## Process

1. Reconcile all gains and property computations.
2. Complete foreign and FA schedules honestly.
3. Pay self-assessment tax if due; file ITR-2, verify in time.

## Fees (indicative, excl. GST 18%)

| Type | Band (INR) |
|------|------------|
| Salary plus simple CG | 2,000 to 5,000 |
| Many properties and CG | 5,000 to 12,000 |
| NRI and foreign | 10,000 to 30,000 |
| High income plus AL | 8,000 to 20,000 |

## Deadlines

Like other non-audit individuals, often 31 July; belated 31 December; audit cases 31 October if you have audit, else follow your category.

## Common errors

- ITR-1 with any capital gain.
- Thin FA for foreign accounts.
- Wrong equity LTCG base after January 2018 rules.

## Frequently asked questions

**NRI, India rent only?** India-source income in ITR-2; FA may still apply for residents; NRI test fact-specific.

**House sold after six years?** ITR-2 Schedule CG, indexation or options as law gives.

**F and O with salary?** ITR-3, not 2.

**Grandfathering equity LTCG?** Follow broker and Section 112A rules in force for the year.
