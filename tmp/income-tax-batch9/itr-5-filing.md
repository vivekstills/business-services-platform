# ITR-5 form filing

## Overview

ITR-5 is for partnership firms, LLPs, AOP, BOI, co-operative society, and similar non-corporate non-individual assessees, not for companies or 139 4A to 4D cases that use ITR-7. It includes full financials, partners or members, 40B limits, and 44AB when threshold hits.

## Features

- P and L, balance sheet, partner capital, 3CD when audit; AMT for LLP where applicable; firm tax rate 30% plus surcharges in usual pattern.

## Documents

P and L, balance sheet, deed or LLP agreement, 3CD, 26AS, GST, capital accounts, TDS, gains if any.

## Process

1. Close books; audit if 44AB; compute firm total income; pay tax; ITR-5 with DSC in firm cases as portal requires.

## Deadlines

31 July or 31 October with tax audit; revised by 31 December in typical years.

## Common errors

- 40B salary over limits.
- Dormant firm not filing nil return.
- Skipping AMT for LLP when triggered.

## Frequently asked questions

**Dormant LLP?** File nil ITR-5; still MCA duties.

**40B limits?** On book profit slabs in section; must match deed.

**LLP Act audit same as 44AB?** No, different law and form.
