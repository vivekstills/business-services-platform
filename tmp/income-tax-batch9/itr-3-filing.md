# ITR-3 form filing

## Overview

ITR-3 is for individuals and HUFs with **business** or **profession** income, with or without salary, house property, and capital gains. It includes freelancers, professionals with books, F and O and intraday traders, partners in firms, and any case not covered by ITR-4 presumption. Full books path means P and L, balance sheet, and Schedule BP as required.

## Applicability (summary)

Business or profession, partner remuneration or interest, F and O as non-speculative business, tax audit 44AB when triggered, high receipts above presumptive limits, or opt out of 44AD or 44ADA.

## Key features

- P and L, balance sheet, BP, loss carry, speculative split, partner schedules.
- Tax audit 3CD link when 44AB applies; DSC often used in audit cases.

## Benefits

- Loss carry forward for business; F and O set-off rules; alignment with 3CD.

## Documents

P and L, balance sheet, 26AS, Form 16 if salary, capital gains, GST, bank, audit report if any, partner documents.

## Process

1. Close books, reconcile GST and income.
2. Get 44AB audit if applicable before due date of return in audit year.
3. Build BP and compute total tax; file ITR-3; verify.

## Fees (excl. GST 18%)

| Case | Band (INR) |
|------|------------|
| Simple freelancer | 3,000 to 8,000 |
| F and O and salary | 5,000 to 15,000 |
| With 44AB | 20,000 to 75,000 |

## Deadlines

31 July for many non-audit; 31 October when tax audit applies; belated 31 December in many years.

## Common errors

- ITR-4 when full books and higher turnover require ITR-3.
- Mixing intraday and F and O in one wrong line.
- Skipping 44AB when due.

## Frequently asked questions

**Doctor, salary and clinic?** ITR-3, or 44ADA ITR-4 if eligible and you elect presumption.

**F and O loss vs salary?** Set-off rules are strict; carry forward in CFL when allowed.

**Shop forty lakh?** 44AD ITR-4 may work; ITR-3 if opt out or facts need it.

**Two partnerships?** Exempt share in EI, taxable salary and interest in BP.

**E-commerce over one crore?** 44AB and digital ten crore exception need study; 31 October return if audit.
