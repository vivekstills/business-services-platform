# ITR-7 form filing

## Overview

ITR-7 is for persons filing under sections 139 4A through 4D: trusts, institutions, political parties, funds, and other notified filers, not for ordinary business companies unless they fall in these clauses.

## Features

- Application of income, accumulation, 10B or 10BB audit, 80G donor data, FCRA style disclosure where relevant, section 11 and 12 compliance.

## Documents

Trust deed, 12A 12AB, 10B, income and spending, donation register, Form 10 for accumulation, FCRA if foreign funds.

## Process

1. Complete audit; build application versus eighty five percent test; file ITR-7; verify with DSC or allowed mode.

## Fees (excl. GST 18%)

| Type | Band (INR) |

| Mid | 15,000 to 40,000 |

## Common errors

- Under application of income; anonymous donation tax; 13 violations; filing before audit when audit is due.

## Frequently asked questions

**Foreign grant?** FCRA and ITR both; characterise corpus versus programme.

**Eighty five percent test failed?** Tax on shortfall or use accumulation in Form 10 with rules.

**Section 8 company?** 12A then ITR-7, else ITR-6 on profit.
