# ITR-6 form filing

## Overview

ITR-6 is for all companies under the Companies Act except those filing ITR-7 for charity. It is data heavy: financials, tax audit 3CA 3CD for all companies, MAT, transfer pricing and 3CEB when applicable, DSC signing mandatory.

## Features

- MAT 115JB, DTA, credit carry, depreciation as per Income-tax Rules, 43B and related chapters.

## Documents

Statutory and tax audit, 29B when needed, 26AS, challans, director list, TP report if applicable.

## Process

1. Complete statutory and tax audits; TP by November 30 in TP cases; compute tax, MAT, credits; file ITR-6 with DSC.

## Deadlines

31 October, or 30 November in international transaction cases that extend due date; check law each year.

## Common errors

- MAT on wrong book profit; wrong depreciation; missing 3CEB when liable.

## Frequently asked questions

**Loss company?** Still file; carry forward losses and unabsorbed depreciation per rules.

**Refund in ITR-6?** Excess TDS and advance can be claimed as usual.

**OPC?** Same company compliance as other private companies.
