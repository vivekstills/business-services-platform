# GST annual return (GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C)

## Overview

**GSTR-9** is the annual return for regular taxpayers. It consolidates the year’s GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and the ITC story (including GSTR-2B-anchored data) into one return covering outward supplies, inward and ITC, tax paid, and year-end adjustments. It is **filed** on **gst.gov.in,** in **principle** by **31** **December** **of** the **next** **FY** (e.g. **FY** **24-25** by **31** **Dec** **25),** but **you** should **re-read** the **last** **CBIC/portal** **extension** **circular** **each** **year.** **GSTR-9C** is a **reconciliation** **of** **GSTR-9** to **audited** **books** and is **tied** to **higher** **turnover;** the **form** and **self-****cert** **model** have **moved** over time, so **lock** the **right** **year** **on** the **portal.**

## Key features (summary)

| Taxpayer band | GSTR-9 / 9C (typical pattern; verify notification) |
|--------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| **ATO** up to **₹2** **cr** | **GSTR-9** **optional;** many still file |
| **Above** **₹2** **cr** | **GSTR-9** **mandatory** (regular, not in excluded class) |
| **Above** **₹5** **cr** | **GSTR-9C**-type **recon** in **vogue**; **who** must **self-cert** v. **deeper** table **is** on **the** **law** **in** **force** for **that** **year** |
| **Composition** | **GSTR-9A,** not **9** (check **if** you **moved** **mid-year**) |
| **ISD,** **NRI** **NRT,** some **TDS** **roles** | **9** per **exemption** list |

## Benefits

- **Closes** the **year** in **one** **return** and **can** **surface** **one-time** **pay** and **amend** before **it** is **late**
- **Forces** **a** **full** **ITC** and **9C**-style **bridge** to **audited** **numbers** for **bigger** **assessees**
- **Helps** **lenders,** **IPO,** and **M&A** **diligence** on **a** **single** **FY** **GST** **store**

## Process

1. **File** all **1** and **3B** for the **year**; **2B/ITR**-style **catch-up** first.
2. **Recon** **sales** **v.** **1** and **2B/books** v. **3B.**
3. **Fill** **9** **tables** (outward, **ITC,** **reversals,** **paid,** **prior** **year** **adjustments).**
4. If **5** **cr+** **recon,** do **9C** **reconciliation** **working** to **P&L.**
5. **Pay** **extra** **tax** and **DRC-03/****ledger** as **advised,** then **file** and **ARN.**

## Applicability

**Regular** **persons** **over** the **2** **cr** **floor** in **an** **FY**; **5** **cr** **PAM**-level **9C**; **each** **GSTIN** **a** **separate** **9,** with **PAM** **across** **all** **GSTINs** of **one** **PAN** for **threshold,** on **advice.**

## Common mistakes (short)

- **Filing** **9** **before** **all** **3B**
- **Forgetting** **10** and **11** for **amend** **to** **other** **years**
- **9C** **turn** **mismatch** **v.** **audited** **P&L** without **a** **bridge**
- **Not** **paying** **DRC-03** when **9** **finds** **more** **tax**

## Frequently asked questions

**Five** **GSTINs,** one **2** **cr** at **PAM,** but **per**-**state** **small?** If **PAM** **2** **cr+,** **all** those **in** that **PAM** **set** that **are** **regular** and **in** **scope** need **9**; **9** is **per** **GSTIN.**
