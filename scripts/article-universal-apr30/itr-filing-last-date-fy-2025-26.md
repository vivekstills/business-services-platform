## Due Date Summary

| Taxpayer Category | Due Date |
|---|---|
| Individuals, HUFs, non-audit firms | **31 July 2026** |
| Audit cases (businesses/professionals above threshold) | **31 October 2026** |
| Transfer pricing cases | **30 November 2026** |
| Revised return | 31 December 2026 |
| Belated return | 31 December 2026 |
| Updated return (ITR-U) | Within 2 years from end of AY |

> 📌 Dates above follow standard statutory provisions. CBDT may issue extensions — always verify on incometax.gov.in.

## Consequences of Missing the Deadline

> ⚠️ **Late filing fee — Section 234F:**
> - Total income ≤ ==Rs. 5 lakh== → ==Rs. 1,000==
> - Total income > ==Rs. 5 lakh== → ==Rs. 5,000==

Beyond the fee:

- Most losses (capital, business) **cannot be carried forward** if ITR is filed late
- Interest under **Section 234A** accrues on unpaid tax from the due date
- Refunds are delayed significantly
- Increased risk of scrutiny notices

## Who Must File Even Below Taxable Limit

Filing is mandatory regardless of income if you:

- Hold foreign assets or have foreign income
- Have TDS deducted and want refund
- Deposited > ==Rs. 1 crore== in bank accounts
- Paid > ==Rs. 2 lakh== in foreign travel
- Paid electricity bills > ==Rs. 1 lakh==

## Compliance Tips

> ✅ File by **31 July 2026** where applicable to preserve **loss carry-forward** and faster refunds.

> ✅ For audit cases, ensure Form **3CA/3CB/3CD** is uploaded by **30 September 2026** before the October ITR deadline.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. If I miss 31 July, can I still file?**
Yes — as a belated return by 31 December 2026, with late fee under 234F. After 31 December, only an Updated Return (ITR-U) with additional tax is possible.

**Q2. No tax is due — can I skip filing?**
If income exceeds the basic exemption limit, filing is mandatory even with zero tax liability. Below that, it is optional but advisable for refund claims and loan/visa documentation.

**Q3. My accounts need audit. When must the audit report be filed?**
The audit report (Form 3CA/3CB + 3CD) must be uploaded by **30 September 2026**, before the 31 October ITR deadline for audit cases.
