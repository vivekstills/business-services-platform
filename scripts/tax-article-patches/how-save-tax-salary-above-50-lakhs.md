If taxable income is **Rs. 50.5 lakh**, surcharge applies — **marginal relief** ensures the **incremental tax** from crossing Rs. 50 lakh does not exceed the **incremental income** beyond Rs. 50 lakh (subject to statutory computation). Always verify with official calculator.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Does the new regime also levy surcharge above Rs. 50 lakh?**
**Yes** — surcharge applies in **both** regimes; ultra-high bands differ (new regime caps lower at extreme incomes).

**Q2. Should I always push income below Rs. 50 lakh?**
Only if legal & cash-flow sensible — **marginal relief** may already soften small breaches.

**Q3. Are 80G donations unlimited?**
**No** — percentage of qualifying donation & adjusted gross total income caps apply per category.
