## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Is forming a HUF always legal tax saving?**
Only with **authentic** corpus & income; GAAR / anti-abuse provisions penalize artificial diversion.

**Q2. Do political donations save tax like 80G?**
Political donations may use **Section 80GGC** with separate eligibility — do not mix regimes.

**Q3. Should Rs. 1Cr+ earners ignore old regime entirely?**
**No** — high deductions (loan, donations, employer NPS) may still dominate; **model both**.
