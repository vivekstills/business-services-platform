## Employer NPS — 80CCD(2)

Employer contributions to NPS can qualify under **80CCD(2)** up to **10% of salary (Basic + DA)** for government / **14%/10%** rules for PSU/private per statute — **without consuming your Rs. 1.5 lakh 80C room**. At Rs. 15L packages, negotiating this component is often the highest ROI conversation with payroll.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Should I always pick old regime at Rs. 15 lakh?**
No — if you rent minimally, have **no loan**, and low deductions, **new regime** may still win.

**Q2. Does 80CCD(2) require my own NPS contribution?**
Employer contribution follows payroll policy; employee voluntary tier is separate — confirm with HR + CA.

**Q3. Can both spouses claim home loan benefits?**
Yes if **co-owners & co-borrowers**, split per ownership share subject to individual caps.
