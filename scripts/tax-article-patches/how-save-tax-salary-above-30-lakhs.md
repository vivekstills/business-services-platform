## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Is surcharge relevant at Rs. 30 lakh?**
Generally **no** — surcharge tiers start **above Rs. 50 lakh** (individual rules).

**Q2. Should I still invest in ELSS at this income?**
Use ELSS if **goal-fit**; tax is a side benefit — avoid duplication if EPF already fills 80C.

**Q3. Can capital losses help here?**
**STCL / LTCL** rules may offset gains — track AIS / broker statements before March 31.
