## Overview

Every income tax filing in India revolves around two time periods that are easy to confuse: the **Financial Year (FY)** and the **Assessment Year (AY)**. Getting them wrong leads to filing under the wrong year, incorrect challan payments, and defective returns.

| Term | Definition | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Financial Year (FY) | The 12-month period in which income is earned | FY 2025-26 = 1 April 2025 to 31 March 2026 |
| Assessment Year (AY) | The 12-month period immediately after the FY in which that income is assessed and taxed | AY 2026-27 = 1 April 2026 to 31 March 2027 |

> **The simple rule:** Income earned in FY 2025-26 is reported and taxed in AY 2026-27.

## Why This Distinction Matters

The Income Tax Department issues all notices, assessments, refunds, and demands in reference to the **Assessment Year**. When you:
- File your ITR, you file for AY 2026-27 (for income earned in FY 2025-26)
- Pay advance tax, you pay for the FY in which you earn
- Receive a refund, it references the AY

Confusing the two when filling challans is one of the most common errors — the payment goes to the wrong year and creates a mismatch.

## Previous Year vs. Assessment Year

The Income Tax Act also uses the term **Previous Year**, which means the same as Financial Year — it is the year in which income is "previously" earned, before it is assessed. So:

- **Previous Year = Financial Year** (same thing, different term)
- The Act uses "previous year" in statutory language; common usage prefers "financial year"

## Common Mistakes

> ⚠️ **Challan error:** When paying self-assessment tax, always select AY (not FY) on the challan. If your income was earned in FY 2025-26, select **AY 2026-27** on Form ITNS 280.

> ⚠️ **ITR form selection:** When filing on the income tax portal, the form asks for AY. Select AY 2026-27 for FY 2025-26 income.

> ⚠️ **Notice responses:** If you receive a notice for AY 2024-25, it refers to income you earned in FY 2023-24.

## Practical Example

Ravi received his salary of Rs. 8 lakh throughout FY 2025-26 (April 2025 to March 2026). He files his ITR in July 2026.
- **FY (Previous Year):** 2025-26 — the year Ravi earned the income
- **AY:** 2026-27 — the year the ITR is filed and the income is assessed
- **Portal selection:** AY 2026-27
- **Challan for advance tax paid in March 2026:** AY 2026-27

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. If I'm paying tax in April 2026 for income earned in FY 2025-26, which AY do I select?**
Select AY 2026-27. The assessment year always follows the financial year, regardless of when you actually pay the tax.

**Q2. Can a notice come for an old AY?**
Yes. The Income Tax Department can issue notices for up to 3–6 years (depending on type) after the relevant AY. For example, a notice in 2026 may refer to AY 2020-21.

**Q3. Does the April 1 start date ever change?**
No. India's Financial Year always runs from 1 April to 31 March. The government has discussed changing this in the past, but the FY/AY structure remains unchanged.
